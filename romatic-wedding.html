ROMANTIC WEDDING

Your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life, an occasion to create lifelong memories with family and friends.
 
Whether it’s a grand affair straight out of a fairy tale in our sumptuous Grand Salon or a simple yet beautiful barefoot ceremony on our own private beach under a gauzy arch, The IMPERIAL Hotel can help make your dream wedding into a reality. You bring the dress and let us take care of the rest!
 
Indoor venues:
·      The Grand Salon (540 sq m)
·      Salon A (360 sq m) or Salon B (180 sq m)
·      Foyer with natural light from a domed skylight two stories above a marble fountain
·      Asian Selection Restaurant (ideal for buffet meals)
 
Outdoor venues:
·      The lush Royal Gardens next to our gorgeous freeform pool (ideal for BBQs)
·      The IMPERIAL Beach Club
·      The IMPERIAL’s private beach
·      Hai Vuong Restaurant (ideal for smaller groups)
 
Available services and facilities:
·      10 set menus featuring both Asian and Western favorites to suit every budget
·      Fully customizable menus
·      Option for semi- or full-buffet meals
·      Flowers
·      Master of Ceremonies
·      Decorations in five color schemes
·      Live piano music
·      And much more…
 
Schedule a free consultation with our dedicated wedding planning team to craft your perfect Special Day.
